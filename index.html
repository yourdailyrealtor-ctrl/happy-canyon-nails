Our Services

Every service is done with care and attention. No rushing, no shortcuts.

💅

Gel Nails

Long-lasting gel polish over natural nails or extensions. Stays glossy and chip-free for 2–3 weeks. Great for low-maintenance clients who still want a polished look.

★ Most Popular
💅

Acrylic Nails

Full sets and fills. Custom lengths from natural to dramatic, shaped and filed to your preference every time. Built to hold up to daily life.

✨

Dip Powder

Stronger than regular polish, lighter than acrylic. No UV lamp required. Hundreds of color options, lasts 3–5 weeks with proper care.

★ Staff Favorite
🌴

Classic Pedicure

Soak, exfoliate, trim, shape, and polish. The foundation done properly. Clean, well-maintained feet without unnecessary add-ons.

🌴

Spa & Luxury Pedicures

Hot stone massage, paraffin wax treatment, extended massage time. The kind of pedicure you genuinely don’t want to end.

★ Client Favorite
💃

Classic Manicure

Hand soak, cuticle care, shaping, and your choice of polish. A clean manicure done the right way — not rushed to the next client.

Nail Art
