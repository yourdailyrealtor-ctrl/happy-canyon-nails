What We Offer

Our Services

Every service is done with attention and care. No rushing, no shortcuts.

💅

Gel Nails

Long-lasting gel polish applied over natural nails or extensions. Stays glossy and chip-free for 2–3 weeks. Great for clients who want low maintenance with a polished look.

★ Most Popular
💅

Acrylic Nails

Full sets and fills. Custom lengths from natural to dramatic. Acrylic that holds up to daily wear. Shaped and filed to your preference every time.

✨

Dip Powder

The best of both worlds — stronger than regular polish, lighter than acrylic. No UV lamp needed. Hundreds of color options. Lasts 3–5 weeks.

★ Staff Favorite
🌴

Classic Pedicure

Soak, exfoliate, trim, shape, and polish. The foundation done properly. Ideal if you just want clean, well-maintained feet without the extras.

🌴

Spa & Luxury Pedicures

Upgraded pedicure experience with hot stone massage, paraffin wax treatment, and extended massage time. The kind of pedi you don’t want to end.

★ Client Favorite
💃

Classic Manicure

Hand soak, cuticle care, shaping, and a polish of your choice. A clean, classic manicure done the right way — not rushed.

🎨

Nail Art

From subtle accents to full custom designs. Bring a photo from Pinterest, Instagram — wherever. The team can recreate it or riff on it. No idea too intricate.

★ Book Ahead
💊

Nail Removal & Repairs

Safe removal of gel, acrylic, or dip powder. Single nail repairs done right, so the fix actually matches the rest. No damage to your natural nails.
